Hair Fall Treatment at Dr. Devendra Rathode

Can hair loss be reversed or stopped? Will my lost hair ever grow back?
These questions plague many people who suffer from hair loss problems. Hair plays an essential role in our overall appearance. Therefore, people suffering from different types of hair loss, often find themselves stressed about their increasing hair fall.
If you too are troubled with these questions and are looking for sustainable solutions that can stop your hair fall or help in hair regrowth, then you’re on the right page.
To begin with, it is important to understand that hair loss is a medical condition and not a cosmetic problem. There are more than 40 different types of hair loss. To ensure you get the best hair fall treatment to enable hair regrowth, it is essential to identify the root cause of your hair loss. This is where homeopathy comes into the picture. Homeopathic hair loss treatment addresses all underlying factors responsible for hair loss conditions and gently aid in hair regrowth.

Male Pattern Blandness

Male pattern baldness, also called androgenic alopecia, is the most common type of hairfall in men. 'Androgenetic' refers to genes, meaning the condition is inherited and ‘alopecia’ refers to hair loss.

According to the American Hair Loss Association, around two-thirds of men will begin to suffer from male pattern hair loss by the age of 35. Moreover, 85% of the global population will eventually suffer from hair loss by the age of 50.

It usually takes 15-25 years for men to go bald, but in some cases, it can occur quicker. Typically, men first witness thinning hair at the sides (temples) and on the top of the head. A bald patch develops gradually in the middle of the scalp. The receding sides and the bald patch on the crown gradually enlarge leaving a patch at the front.

Even though male pattern baldness is a hereditary phenomenon, you can certainly prevent or delay it with the right treatment. At Dr Batra's™, Geno Homeopathy (a gene-targeted homeopathic therapy) can be advised for such cases. A simple saliva test can help us determine your genetic disposition and help to reduce your chances of suffering from hair loss. The sooner you start with the treatment, the better are your chances of recovery. An integrated approach to the treatment as well as choosing the right combination of treatment for hereditary hair loss will certainly be of help.

Losing your hair isn’t the same as men balding. Male pattern hair loss is a genetic condition whereas hair thinning can be caused by a variety of factors. These factors can be thyroid disorders, diabetes, medications, smoking and even stress. Before jumping into conclusions, it is advised to consult a homeopathic doctor for an effective hair fall treatment.
